Rewrite the resume for one selected role with these constraints:

- Keep every fact truthful.
- Do not invent employers, dates, achievements, metrics, certifications, or tools.
- Surface the strongest JD-aligned evidence first in the summary, experience ordering, and bullet ordering.
- Mirror the job-description language only where the resume provides evidence.
- Prefer bullets that show ownership, outcomes, and measurable impact.
- Reduce irrelevant or generic wording when stronger role-specific evidence exists.
- Preserve markdown output and keep the resume scannable for an ATS and recruiter.

Prioritize:

1. Role title and seniority fit.
2. Required skills and tools.
3. Domain and problem-space alignment.
4. Evidence of scope, impact, and collaboration.
5. Location, remote, or hiring constraints if the JD makes them important.
