Write a one-page cover letter with this structure:

1. Opening:
State one concrete reason the role fits the candidate. Reference something specific from the JD.

2. Evidence paragraph:
Highlight the most relevant experience and outcomes using 2-3 sentences.

3. Company-specific paragraph:
Explain why this company or role context is a fit. Avoid generic praise.

4. Closing:
Use a direct and confident close with a clear interview ask.

Style rules:

- Sound professional and human.
- Avoid generic enthusiasm phrases.
- Avoid filler like "team player", "hard worker", or "passionate about".
- Keep claims grounded in the source resume.
- Prefer specificity over flourish.
