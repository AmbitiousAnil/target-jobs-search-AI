Before finalizing the application package, verify:

- The tailored resume does not invent experience or qualifications.
- The first half of the resume clearly reflects the role's main requirements.
- Important JD keywords are mirrored only where supported by the resume.
- The cover letter references the role and company specifically.
- The cover letter contains at least one concrete evidence statement from the candidate background.
- The tone is direct and confident, not generic or overly eager.
- File outputs are clean markdown with no preamble or meta commentary.
